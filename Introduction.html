Objective: Explore the ruins

Introduction:

The Stormlight Archive is the story of Roshar, a world of storms and war. It is a story of growth, passion, and honor. Today, it is a story you will tell. You are not heroes. You currently live in a war-camp at the edge of the Shattered Planes, a vast expanse of chasms and plateaus. Today, you're traveling with soldiers and scholars loyal to an Alethi noble named highprince Sadeas. That is where our story begins. A few days ago, a highstorm uncovered ruins dating back to the Silver Kingdoms. Now you're part of an expedition sent across the barren stone to investigate what's left of those ruins.
