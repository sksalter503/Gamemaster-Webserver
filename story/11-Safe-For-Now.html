11 - Safe For Now

Once all surviving characters have made it through the crevice, they are safe. If the chasmfiend failed to kill any of the player characters, read the following:

Here at the narrowest point of the chasm, the sheer cliff walls are about 5 feet apart. The chasmfiend pushes its head into the gap, but withdraws a few tense moments later, unable to squeeze through. Ahead, the chasm widens again.

The chasmfiend tries to claw at any character who lingers in the gap, but does not push through. About halfway up the chasm, the gap widens to 10 feet, but the chasmfiend is too injured to climb.

Past the narrow crevice, the chasm opens up to safety. If Eliah Matal is still alive, she urgest the team onward. Checking her map, she determines that it's an hour's hike to another branch in the chasm. (If Matal and her map are lost, the characters can determine this by simply heading onward.) By the end of the hike, the sun sets.

The chasm continues to the northeast. The surface is still 80 feet above you, with little in the way of handholds between you and the evening sky. On the east side of the canyon, about 10 feet up the wall, there's a small cave.
